Sign-up page

  • Username needs to be between 8 - 20 characters.

  • Password must be between 8 - 20 characters.

  • Must contain at least 1 number, 1 uppercase letter and 1 symbol.

  • Not repeat the same number or letter more than 3 times in a row.

  • Special characters/symbols not allowed: @#*()+={}/?~,.-_

  • Make sure your password matches.

  • Make sure your email is a valid email.
